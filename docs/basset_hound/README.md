# Basset Hound Theme

A warm, loyal, and gentle theme inspired by the droopy, lovable basset hound.

## Overview

The Basset Hound theme brings a cozy, patient, and loyal energy to your money management. With warm brown and cream tones, this theme is all about going at your own pace and celebrating steady progress.

## Visual Style

- **Color Palette**: Warm browns, creams, and tans
- **Font**: Garamond serif (classic and comforting)
- **Mood**: Relaxed, loyal, steady, patient
- **Vibe**: Long ears, slow pace, big heart

## Image Requirements

Place 10 images in this folder following this naming convention:

```
basset_hound/1.jpg
basset_hound/2.jpg
basset_hound/3.jpg
basset_hound/4.jpg
basset_hound/5.jpg
basset_hound/6.jpg
basset_hound/7.jpg
basset_hound/8.jpg
basset_hound/9.jpg
basset_hound/10.jpg
```

**Image Ideas:**
- Basset hounds in various poses (sitting, lying down, walking)
- Close-ups of droopy ears and sad/loyal eyes
- Basset hounds in cozy environments
- Basset hounds with their people
- Sleeping basset hounds
- Basset hounds outdoors on leisurely walks

**Technical Requirements:**
- Format: JPEG or PNG
- Recommended size: 500-600px width
- Aspect ratio: Landscape (3:2 or similar) works best
- File size: Reasonable for web (under 500KB each)

## Theme Messages

The theme uses warm, encouraging language with a loyal dog personality:

- **Start messages**: "No rush. One paw in front of the other."
- **Step messages**: "Atta boy. Keep going."
- **Final messages**: "All done, buddy. You did it."

All messages remind you that steady progress is good progress, and you're never alone in this.
