# How to Do the Bills

## 1) Do the Math First

> Do this before moving money, or you may not have enough to cover bills.

### Inputs
- Current **VyStar checking** balance
- Current **VyStar savings** balance
- Current **Citi** balance
- Keep **$4,000** in checking for automatic bills

### Formula

$$
	ext{result} = \text{checking} - \text{citi} - 4000
$$

### What `result` Means
- **If `result` is negative**: You do not have enough in checking.
  - Move money **from savings to checking**.
- **If `result` is positive**: You have extra money this month.
  - Check savings balance:
    - If savings is **under $70,000**, move `result` to **savings**.
    - If savings is **$70,000 or more**, move `result` to **Vanguard**.

---

## 2) Pay Bills and Move Money

1. Move money to **savings** if `result` is positive and savings is under $70,000.
2. Move money from **savings to checking** if `result` is negative.
3. Pay the **Citi credit card**. It is usually due by the 9th, but should be payed closer to the 1st.
4. Move money to **Vanguard** if `result` is positive and savings is at least $70,000.
